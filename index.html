AMR DEE HUNCHO

Background

Saint Louis has a rich musical history. From the soulful sounds of Ike and Tina Turner to the chart topping albums from the Saint Lunatics and Chingy. Saint Louis is a powerhouse full of musical talent. One such talented artist is a rapper who goes by the name Amr Dee Huncho. His level of artistry is above average.

Amr Dee Huncho grew up on the Northside of Saint Louis as a youth. He later moved to south Saint Louis. This is where he began to grow as an artist. He has put out several mixtapes, and many of his songs have reached a million views on Youtube.

Future of STL Rap

As of late, music has reflected the environment of society. Saint Louis's rap scene is no different. It reflects the culture of Saint Louis, particlularly the inner city. The stories reflect the violent nature of the streets. Streets that are filled with hoplessness, poverty, and dispair. A place where dreams are 'sold' to eager buyers, who oftimes get scammed out of their freedsom, and sometimes their lives. In 2022, Amr Dee Huncho was a victim of such violence. He was shot several times while sitting in a vehicle. There were even reports that he had passed. Thankfully these rumors were false, and he bounced back. His music documents these occurences in his life. With this theme, let's wish for new levels for bro!

The future of Stl Rap is a bright one. With artists such as Amr Dee Hunch, the creativing and sound is steadily evolving.

There are other Stl artists that are making their mark in music. A few of these artists are 3 Problems, La 4ss, and Htman Holla. Hitman Holla is on the nationally sindicated show 'Wildin Out', where he does raps to a live audience.
